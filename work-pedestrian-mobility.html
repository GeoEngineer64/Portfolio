Woodburn Village Pedestrian Mobility Analysis


November 2022
Engineering|Analysis

This report collected and analyzed issues with accessible pedestrian infrastructure in and around the Woodburn Village condominium community in Annandale, VA. Though not a full scale in depth analysis of the issues within the community's pedestrian infrastructure, this study provides a starting point for further investigation on the matter to upgrade and enhance the mobility of the community for all residents.
